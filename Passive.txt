Passive: Power of Space

QianZong collects Space Debris from killing enemies which upgrades his abilities.
When QianZong reaches level 16, Space Debris exceeding 300 are converted into ability power.
When QianZong has collected more than 400 Space Debris, Spacetime Rift and Spacetime Blast deal additional true damage equals to 10% targets' maximum health.

Minions: 1 Space Debris
Cannon minions and non-epic jungle monsters: 5 Space Debris
Enemy champions and epic jungle monsters: 10 Space Debris
